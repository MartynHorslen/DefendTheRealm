# Defend The Realm

A checkpoint-based tower defence game set in the same universe as *Conquer The Realm*.

Where CTR is about expanding and conquering, Defend The Realm is about holding the line — building a maze of towers, managing a power network, and surviving increasingly brutal waves of enemies.

## What Makes This Different

Most tower defence games give you a fixed path and ask you to place towers alongside it. Defend The Realm inverts this: the map has mandatory checkpoints enemies must visit in order, but the *route between them* is determined by where you place your towers. Build walls of towers to force enemies on the longest possible walk before they reach each checkpoint.

The power network adds a second layer of strategy. Towers don't run on their own — they need power routed to them via generators and connector chains. Too many towers on one circuit means each fires slower. Managing your power grid is as important as managing your maze.

## Universe

Defend The Realm shares lore, factions, and visual language with *Conquer The Realm*. Units, building styles, and elemental forces will be consistent across both games.
